I'm a robotics and mechanical engineer at Honeybee Robotics, a Blue Origin company, specializing in space mechanisms, deployable structures, and robotic systems integration. My work spans the full lifecycle — from requirements definition and trade studies through detailed design, prototyping, testing, and field deployments.

I've led multi-disciplinary teams and managed $3M+ budgets across 15+ concurrent R&D programs. On the hardware side I focus on mechanism design, GD&T, and thermal systems. On the software side I build real-time motor control systems in C++ and Python. I care about the intersection of elegant engineering and real-world reliability — whether that's on the surface of Mars or a glacier in the Canadian Arctic.
